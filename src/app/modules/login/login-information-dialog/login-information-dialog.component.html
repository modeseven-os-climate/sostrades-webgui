For you first connection on the {{data.platform}} platform

After clicking on the GitHub icon, you will be redirected to an authorization page like the image below.

You need to click the button "Authorize b4pm-devops", and then your GitHub account will be associated with our platform.

Then, you ou will be able to log in.

To create a SoSTrades account, please contact the sostrades team at {{data.support}} the developper team.

Please provide your first name, last name and email.

Then, a username will be provided with a link to change your password.
